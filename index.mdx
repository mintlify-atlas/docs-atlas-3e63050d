## What is P.FLEX?

P.FLEX is an industrial B2B ERP system designed specifically for flexographic printing plants. It provides real-time production tracking, comprehensive inventory management, quality control, and complete traceability from order intake to finished goods.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Production Tracking" icon="chart-line" href="/features/production">
    Monitor print, diecut, rewind, and packaging operations in real-time with KPIs and metrics
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inventory Management" icon="warehouse" href="/features/inventory">
    Manage clichés, dies, inks, materials, and finished goods with rack layout visualization
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order Management" icon="file-invoice" href="/features/orders">
    Track work orders (OT) from intake through production to completion with full history
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quality Control" icon="badge-check" href="/features/quality">
    Report incidents, track tooling status, and maintain compliance with audit logs
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
